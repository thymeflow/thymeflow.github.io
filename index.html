Features

Thymeflow connects your email, calendar, contacts and location history together to derive new knowledge, answer queries and perform analytics.

Email and Contacts

Thymeflow deduplicates contacts in your address book and email to respond to queries more accurately.

Location

Thymeflow analyzes your location history and extracts places you have been to.

Calendar

Thymeflow links events in your calendar with places you have been to.
