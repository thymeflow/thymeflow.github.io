Thymeflow
A timeline of your life

Features

Thymeflow combines your location, calendar, mails and contact info to link places you have been to with people you have met with and events you have attended.

Mails and Contacts

Thymeflow uses your Mails and Contact list to know know more about your contacts.

Location

Thymeflow creates a history of your Location by recording places you have been to.

Calendar

Thymeflow links events in your calendar with locations and contacts.
