About

Thymeflow is an open-source system that integrates personal data from di fferent sources, allowing global querying and analysis. It combines your location, calendar, email and contacts together to derive new knowledge. Knowledge is represented in an RDF model based on the schema.org ontology.

Thymeflow is a research project conducted by Sinovia, a subsidiary of ENGIE, in collaboration with INRIA.

Thymeflow is committed to keeping your data private by not sharing it with anyone, not even us.
